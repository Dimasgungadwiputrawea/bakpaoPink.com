How to Raise Your Skeletons

(나는 스켈레톤을 키운다)

A necromancer is a profession considered the noble of nobles, with less than 1,000 in the population
worldwide! I was born with a diamond spoon, no, a necromancer spoon in my mouth!
…Or so I thought. 'I’m just a defective necromancer.'
[Using skill, 'Summon Ordinary Skeleton' (E-rank).]
You'd think a necromancer should be able to summon liches,
Dullahans, or death knights. But nope, I can only summon skeletons.
But then one day, suddenly… [Hidden condition achieved!]
[Condition: The 10,000 Uses Rule] [Your rank has upgraded to D-rank!] I achieved
the hidden condition and gained the ability to raise my skeletons. From E-rank to S-rank!
The story of Joo Donghoon unfolds, the world's only cursed necromancer,
who’s speeding toward becoming a ranker!

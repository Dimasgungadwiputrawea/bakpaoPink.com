The Novel's Extra

In a world where portals connect Earth to dangerous dungeons teeming with monsters, humanity relies on Hunters—individuals with superhuman abilities—to protect them. Among these Hunters, Sung Jin-Woo is infamously known as the "World's Weakest," barely scraping by in low-level raids.

Monarch Of Dead

However, everything changes when a seemingly routine dungeon exploration turns deadly. Betrayed by his team and left to die, Jin-Woo awakens to a mysterious "Player System" that allows him to level up infinitely, breaking the limits of any known Hunter. As he grows stronger, Jin-Woo uncovers the dark secrets behind the dungeons and his newfound power.

Surviving Broken Hero

Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest Hunter, barely survives low-level dungeons until a deadly raid unlocks a mysterious "Player System." Now able to level up without limits, he grows stronger, uncovering the secrets behind the dungeons and his powers. From underdog to unstoppable, Jin-Woo's rise reshapes the fate of humanity in this thrilling action-packed manhwa.
